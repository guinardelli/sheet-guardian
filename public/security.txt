# Security Policy

If you discover a security vulnerability, please report it to us at security@sheetguardian.com

Please include:
- Description of the vulnerability
- Steps to reproduce
- Potential impact
- Your contact information (optional)

We take security seriously and will respond to all valid reports within 48 hours.

# Bug Bounty
Currently, we do not have a formal bug bounty program, but we appreciate responsible disclosure
and will acknowledge security researchers who report valid vulnerabilities.
